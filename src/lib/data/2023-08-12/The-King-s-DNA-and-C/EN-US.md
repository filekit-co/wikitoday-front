In recent news, a Grade 5 bureaucrat at the Ministry of Education sent a letter to their child&#39;s primary school teacher, causing uproar due to the outrageous demands outlined in the correspondence. The bureaucrat, who revealed their official position in the letter, suggested that their child possesses &#39;royal DNA&#39; and should be treated accordingly, as if they were a prince. The letter also included a threatening tone towards the teacher and mentioned reporting another classroom teacher for child abuse.
