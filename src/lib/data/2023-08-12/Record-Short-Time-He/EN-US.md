# Record-Short-Time Heavy Rainfall Information in Iwate Prefecture

<p class="datetime"><em>2023-08-12 - wikitoday<em></p>

<blockquote class="quote-container dark">
  <p class="quote-text dark">
    The Japan Meteorological Agency has issued a &#39;&#39;Record-breaking Short-Time Heavy Rainfall Information&#39;&#39; for heavy rainfall in Iwate Prefecture. Around Otsuchi Town, it is estimated that approximately 100 millimeters of heavy rain fell in one hour until 7:10 a.m. on January 12. The risk of disasters such as landslides, flooding of houses, and flooding of rivers is increasing. Please pay attention to the evacuation information released by the municipalities and ensure your own safety.
  </p>
</blockquote>
