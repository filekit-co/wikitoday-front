<div class="article-body">
A recent investigation by the BBC has revealed that the gambling industry has been targeting Fantasy Premier League players by sponsoring websites associated with the popular game. The report found evidence of betting firms sponsoring platforms that provide tips, advice, and analysis to players. This raises concerns over the potential influence of such sponsorship on players&#39; decision-making and the overall integrity of the game.
