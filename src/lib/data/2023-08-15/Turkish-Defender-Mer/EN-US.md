<div class="article-body">
Turkish central defender Merih Demiral is currently training alone as he looks for a new club. After spending 22 million euros to redeem him from Juventus, Atalanta is determined to recoup their investment. They have rejected offers from Inter Milan and Roma, demanding a minimum of 20 million euros for Demiral&#39;s transfer.
