<div class="article-body">
Pelageya became a mom in 2017. In her marriage with hockey player Ivan Telegin, the artist gave birth to a daughter Taisia, but to bring up the little girl in a complete family the pop star did not succeed, because soon the athlete left his wife and child for the sake of another woman - Maria Gonchar.

 Divorce artist with her ex-husband did not go smoothly: the couple waged a long war over jointly acquired property, and then - because of child support. The latter issue was resolved only last year, as Telegin demanded to reduce payments on Taisia.

The hockey player pointed out that he is the father of not one, but two children (he had to recognize the son Makar, who was born before his marriage to Pelageya), as a result, he now allocates much less money to the heiress. Seen with the girl Telegin also not often, but Pelageya gives his daughter maximum attention and even takes her to her own performances.

 At one of the last concerts of the artist, held in the Green Theater at VDNH, the girl went on stage with her mother and demonstrated good vocal abilities, impressing the audience. It turned out that Taisia inherited a talent for singing from the performer, so in the future will surely follow in her footsteps.
