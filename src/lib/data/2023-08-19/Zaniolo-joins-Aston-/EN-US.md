<div class="article-body">
Nicolò Zaniolo has left Galatasaray after only six months and has joined Aston Villa on loan in the Premier League. The 24-year-old striker was strongly wanted by Aston Villa&#39;s sporting director Monchi, who had previously worked with him at Roma.


</div>

<div class="article-body">
Zaniolo was close to a move to England back in January but chose to join Galatasaray instead, where he won the Turkish league last season. Aston Villa will pay 5 million euros plus bonuses for the loan, and the redemption fee if exercised will be 22.5 million euros.

 Galatasaray will also receive 10% of Zaniolo&#39;s future resale.
