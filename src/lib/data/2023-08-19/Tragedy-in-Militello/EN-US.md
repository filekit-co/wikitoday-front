<div class="article-body">
During the celebration of the Holy Savior, the town&#39;s patron saint, a man was killed and his wife injured after being hit by a confetti shooter pipe at the exit of a church in the historic center of Militello. The couple was struck by metal parts of the pipe, which is powered by a compressed air system.


</div>

The festivities were promptly suspended, and the Carabinieri arrived at the scene. The incident was filmed by witnesses and shared on social media.

 The Caltagirone Public Prosecutor&#39;s Office has launched an investigation, with the Carabinieri leading the probe.
