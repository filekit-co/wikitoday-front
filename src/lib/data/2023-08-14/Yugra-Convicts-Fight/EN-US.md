<div class="article-body">
There has been a decrease in the number of criminal offenses in public places by 15.7%, on the streets by 9%, and in the residential sector by 7.6%. In terms of street crime, there is a tendency to reduce serious bodily injury by 22.2%, theft by 18.1%, robbery by 28.6%, and other crimes. The police response from Ruslan Kondrashov highlights these statistics.
