Fausto Silva recently stepped down from his role as the host of his show on Band after a year and a half. The network released a statement informing that the daily program will no longer feature Faustão in the second half of the year.

 In his absence, the show is being taken over by his son João Guilherme and presenter Anne Lottermann.
