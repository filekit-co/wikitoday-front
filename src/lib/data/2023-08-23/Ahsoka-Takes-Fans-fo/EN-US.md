<div class="article-body">
Star Wars TV shows have reached a point where franchise fans consume every new one eagerly, but casual viewers are becoming more selective. Ahsoka Tano&#39;s latest space series, titled &#39;Ahsoka&#39;, is no exception.


</div>

<div class="article-body">
While it has glimpses of what made previous Star Wars shows enjoyable, it falls into the trap of taking viewers&#39; interest for granted. The show revolves around Ahsoka Tano, a former apprentice to Anakin Skywalker, on a quest to find and neutralize Grand Admiral Thrawn.

 However, the series suffers from a slow pace, over-reliance on dialogue, and a lack of excitement. Despite its all-female trio and potential for a character-driven narrative, &#39;Ahsoka&#39; fails to deliver a captivating viewing experience.


</div>
