<div class="article-body">
Lorraine is celebrating the success of one of its own as Lou Agathe makes it to the final of The Voice Kids. Lou Agathe&#39;s voice is both delicate and powerful, captivating the judges once again.

 As the only instrumentalist in the competition, the young girl from Sierck-les-Bains has impressed everyone and secured her place in the live final on August 29. When she&#39;s not busy with school or homework, Lou Agathe indulges in her passion for music, playing the guitar and piano.
