Inter Miami CF claimed their first-ever trophy by defeating Nashville SC in a penalty shootout in the Leagues Cup final. Lionel Messi scored a spectacular goal in the 23rd minute, curling a left-footed strike into the top left corner of the net.

 This victory marks a significant milestone for Inter Miami CF and adds to Messi&#39;s decorated career with his 44th trophy.
