<div class="article-body">
Actress Aracy Balabanian died on Monday morning (7) at the age of 83 in Rio de Janeiro. The confirmation was made by Clínica São Vicente, in Gávea, south zone of the city. The cause of death was not revealed.

<div class="article-body">
Balabanian had a career spanning over 50 years, with participation in more than 30 soap operas and plays. She was widely known for her role in the TV show &#39;Sai de Baixo&#39; on TV Globo. Celebrities took to social media to express their condolences and pay tribute to the iconic actress.

 Miguel Falabella, her longtime collaborator, wrote a heartfelt tribute, calling her a great actress and an impeccable professional. Other celebrities such as Claudia Raia, Beth Goulart, and Patrícia Pillar also bid farewell to Balabanian and praised her talent and contributions to Brazilian television. The funeral will be held at the Municipal Theater of Rio, and the actress will be cremated in a closed ceremony for friends and family at Caju Cemetery.
