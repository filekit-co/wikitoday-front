Canadian rapper Tory Lanez, whose legal name is Daystar Peterson, is expected to be sentenced for shooting and wounding fellow artist Megan Thee Stallion in both her feet during an argument. The sentencing process began with Judge David Herriford reading supportive letters and statements from Lanez&#39;s acquaintances, including fellow artists like Iggy Azalea. Megan Thee Stallion, who described being tormented and terrorized, has stated that she cannot be in the same room as Lanez due to his mocking of her trauma.
